Patent Information
The original patent data was taken from http://www.nber.org/patents. For our graph, we used a subset of patent data from the years 1991-1995. 
Each patent is represented as a node in the graph. Citations between patents are represented as the edges of the graph. The graph contains contains 
100000 nodes with 5 dimensions and 188631 edges.
